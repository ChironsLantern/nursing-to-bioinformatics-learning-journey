EL5 Overview

Proteins are the workers of the cellular city:

  • DNA = recipe book
  • mRNA = recipe copy
  • Ribosome = kitchen
  • Amino acids = ingredients
  • Protein = folded worker/tool/robot

Shape = job.

Levels of Structure

  1. Primary: amino acid sequence
  2. Secondary: Œ±-helices & Œ≤-sheets
  3. Tertiary: full 3D fold
  4. Quaternary: multi-subunit assembly

Folding forces:

  • hydrophobic collapse
  • hydrogen bonds
  • electrostatics
  • van der Waals interactions
  • disulfide bonds

Bioinformatics:
AlphaFold2 predicts structure from sequence using evolutionary covariance.

Show EL5 / Med / BIS cheat-note for proteins
  • EL5: Bead strings that fold into oddly specific little robots.
  • Med: Hierarchically organized polymers whose 3D conformation determines biochemical function.
  • BIS: Sequences mapped to structural ensembles using MSAs, coevolution, and ML for functional annotation.

üî• Final Hybrid Summary
